[MEDSCAPE] Medscape Physician Compensation Report
  URL: https://www.medscape.com/sites/public/physician-comp/
  Vintage: 2024 income (Survey Oct 2024 - Jan 2025)
  Sample: 7,300+ physicians across 29+ specialties
  Method: Manual review of public summary tables
  Used for: Average compensation column for all specialties
  Last pulled: 2025-04-15
  Next expected: April 2026

[DOXIMITY] Doximity Physician Compensation Report
  URL: https://www.doximity.com/reports/physician-compensation-report/2025
  PDF: https://assets.doxcdn.com/image/upload/pdfs/doximity-physician-compensation-report-2024.pdf
  Vintage: 2025 report (CY2024 calendar-year data)
  Sample: 37,000+ physician compensation surveys (Jan-Dec 2024)
  Method: Manual review of public PDF
  Used for: Doximity Avg column, top/bottom metro rankings, geographic adjustments
  Last pulled: 2025-04-15
  Next expected: April 2026

[BLS] BLS Occupational Employment and Wage Statistics (OEWS)
  URL: https://www.bls.gov/oes/current/oes_nat.htm
  API: https://api.bls.gov/publicAPI/v2/timeseries/data/
  Vintage: May 2024 (released April 2025)
  Method: Programmatic API fetch by SOC code (29-1211 through 29-1248)
  Used for: BLS W-2 Mean column (employed-only floor)
  Caveat: Excludes self-employed practice owners; mean wage includes base + bonus only
  Last pulled: 2025-04-15
  Next expected: April 2026

[CMS_GPCI] CMS Geographic Practice Cost Index (GPCI)
  URL: https://www.cms.gov/medicare/physician-fee-schedule/search/documentation
  Secondary (full tables): https://www.cgm.com/usa_en/articles/articles/2024-gpci-geographic-practice-cost-indices.html
  Vintage: CY 2024 (effective Jan 1, 2024)
  Conversion factor 2024: $32.7442
  Method: Manual download of CMS PFS files
  Used for: Geographic Adjustment Factor (GAF) in Geographic View tab
  Formula: GAF = Work × 0.5089 + PE × 0.4484 + MP × 0.0428
  Last pulled: 2025-04-15
  Next expected: November 2025 (CY2025 finalized rule)

[CMS_PUF] CMS Medicare Physician & Other Practitioners by Provider & Service
  URL: https://data.cms.gov/provider-summary-by-type-of-service/medicare-physician-other-practitioners
  API: https://data.cms.gov/provider-summary-by-type-of-service/medicare-physician-other-practitioners/medicare-physician-other-practitioners-by-provider-and-service/api
  Vintage: CY2022 (latest released ~Q2 2024)
  Method: Bulk download + aggregation by specialty
  Used for: Specialty-level Medicare share, billing volumes (when aggregated)
  Last pulled: 2025-04-15
  Next expected: Q2 2025 (CY2023 release)

[MEDPAC] MedPAC Data Book
  URL: https://www.medpac.gov/wp-content/uploads/2024/07/July2024_MedPAC_DataBook_SEC.pdf
  Section 10 (Physicians): https://www.medpac.gov/wp-content/uploads/2024/07/July2024_MedPAC_DataBook_Sec10_SEC.pdf
  Vintage: July 2024 (CY2022/2023 claims)
  Method: PDF extraction (manual)
  Used for: Medicare share inputs at specialty group level
  Caveat: Per-specialty Medicare share requires aggregating CMS PUF directly
  Last pulled: 2025-04-15
  Next expected: July 2025

[URBAN] Urban Institute Commercial Markup Study
  URL: https://www.urban.org/research/publication/commercial-health-insurance-markups-over-medicare-prices-physician-services-vary-widely-specialty
  Vintage: 2017-2019 data (structurally stable since publication)
  Method: Manual review of published markup tables
  Used for: Payer mix attractiveness calibration
  Key findings used: Anesthesiology ~330% Medicare; Orthopedics ~150%; Family Med ~110%; Dermatology ~90%
  Last pulled: 2025-04-15
  Next expected: Periodic (no fixed schedule)

[MGMA] MGMA Published Benchmarks
  URL: https://www.mgma.com/articles/foundational-benchmarks-and-kpis-for-medical-practice-operations-in-2023
  KPI article: https://www.mgma.com/articles/foundational-benchmarks-and-kpis-for-medical-practice-operations-in-2023
  Operating costs: https://www.mgma.com/mgma-stat/medical-practice-operating-costs-are-still-rising-in-2025-heres-how-to-control-them
  Provider Comp Report (PDF): https://www.mgma.com/getkaiasset/252744ee-c63b-4a96-9211-8a5d6b908b39/MGMA-2024-Provider-Compensation-Data-Report.pdf
  Vintage: 2023-2024 published figures
  Method: Manual review of public articles + press releases
  Used for: Days-in-AR benchmark (<40 best practice), overhead 60-70% rule of thumb
  Caveat: Specialty-level overhead and AR data is paywalled in MGMA DataDive
  Per-specialty overhead figures shown are derived from public industry sources with ±5pp uncertainty
  Last pulled: 2025-04-15
  Next expected: Ongoing (industry trade publications)

[PAYER_MIX] Per-Specialty Payer Mix Estimates
  Sources combined:
    - AMA 2017 Patient-Mix Benchmark Survey: https://www.ama-assn.org/sites/ama-assn.org/files/corp/media-browser/public/health-policy/PRP-2017-physician-benchmark-survey-patient-mix.pdf
    - PayerPrice industry blog: https://payerprice.com/blog/payer-mix
    - Medical Economics: https://www.medicaleconomics.com/view/physicians-drive-the-health-care-economy-through-commercial-insurance-billing
    - MedPAC age-group utilization curves
  Caveat: Per-specialty payer mix at this granularity is NOT freely published in any single source.
          Estimates here are derived from the above sources with ±10pp uncertainty.
          Always validate against actual borrower payer mix from CFO/practice manager.
  Last pulled: 2025-04-15

[KFF] Kaiser Family Foundation - How Medicare Pays Physicians (reference)
  URL: https://www.kff.org/medicare/what-to-know-about-how-medicare-pays-physicians/
